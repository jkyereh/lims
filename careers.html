Enjoy A Rewarding Career

Take advantage of this opportunity to work in one of the most rewarding positions in your community! We are actively seeking caring individuals who would like to make a difference in their area by working as caregivers for the elderly.


Requirements

  • High school graduation required or GED
  • Certification (HHA, CNA, CMT, PCA, MT)
  • Minimum 1 year experience in a caregiving setting
  • English proficiency
  • Physical ability to support care of elders
  • Good communication skills required (verbal and written)
  • Completion of 12hr aide training by LIMS
  • Criminal background check within 2 years
  • Experience with end of life care, a plus
  • Accommodate to flexible scheduling
  • Perform customized duties depending in patient/family needs
  • Be an active team member in nursing plan of care
  • References requested
