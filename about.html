About Us

LIMS : The Lord Is My Shepherd Home Care LLC

Our History

The Lord Is My Shepherd Home Care LLC, was founded by Samantha Green, originally from Ghana, and a caregiver as well. Samantha is motivated to treat her patients as she would want to be treated when she's incapable.LIMS is founded on Christian practices. With God, love and care, LIMS' team effectively execute LIMS' Mission.


The establishment of LIMS was necessated by the many different challenges our bodies go through when aging. It is obvious that our body looses its grip on many things as we grow, activities and daily routine become difficult and simple household chores seem too big a task. LIMS was initiated for these purposes, to make life easier and worth living for old folks.


Here at LIMS, we take pride in our ethics and values. We provide various services such as: meal prep, transportation, personal care assistance and many more activities for our patients to engage at their leisure.
